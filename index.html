// Tinkering through life.

My journey of playing with the notion of "what if I do this..?" with things started pretty early from what I heard from my parents. One of my favourite toys had broken when I was a kid, it was a RC car. This led me to dig into it with a spare screwdriver and see and learn how it worked with assistance from my dad. This helped me learn what a motor does, what a battery is and that it operated on DC current which also led me to be curious about the link between this DC current and a cassete tape of AC/DC's album my dad had which then got me into music thereby leading to a chain reaction of events that made me the "me" I am today.
